# SessionTracker

A personal tracking app for logging your gambling sessions and managing your bankroll.

## Features

- Track session profit/loss
- Log locations, games, and categories
- Organize sessions by trips
- View summary statistics and charts
- Export data to CSV
- iCloud sync across devices
